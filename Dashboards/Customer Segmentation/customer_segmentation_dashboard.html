Key insights & recommendations

🏆

Protect high-value customers

Just 25% of customers drive 65.6% of all revenue ($135.4M). Prioritise retention, personalisation, and loyalty programs for this cohort first — losing even a small slice here hits harder than losing the entire low-value tier.

🚀

Mid-to-high upgrade is the key lever

24,743 mid-value customers average $2,693 LTV. If just 10% converted to high-value behaviour, it would unlock ~$102M in incremental LTV. This segment is the single biggest untapped growth opportunity.

📊

Audit low-value cost-to-serve

Low-value customers average only $351 LTV — 31× less than high-value. Identify whether they're early-stage with upgrade potential or genuinely low-intent. Reduce acquisition and servicing costs accordingly to protect margins.
